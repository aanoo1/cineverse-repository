我们的使命 | OUR MISSION

CineVerse Classics 是一个为古典电影爱好者创建的在线平台，旨在保护并推广"电影黄金时代"的经典作品。我们相信每一部古典电影都是文化遗产的重要组成部分，值得被新一代观众发现和欣赏。 CineVerse Classics is an online platform created for classic film enthusiasts, dedicated to preserving and promoting masterpieces from the "golden age of cinema". We believe every classic film is an important part of cultural heritage, worthy of discovery and appreciation by new generations of viewers.
